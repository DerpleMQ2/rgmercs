Answers

Question: Why am I not using Dicho spells?

Answer: The [DoDicho] setting determines whether or not your PC will cast Dicho spells. If you are not using Dicho spells, you may need to Enable the [DoDicho] setting.

Settings Used: DoDicho

Question: Why do I not cast any DOTs?

Answer: The [DoDot] setting determines whether or not your PC will cast Damage Over Time spells. If you are not casting any DOTs, you may need to Enable the [DoDot] setting.

Settings Used: DoDot

Question: Why am I not AE slowing?

Answer: The [AESlowCount] setting determines the number of XT Haters before we start AE slowing. If you are not AE slowing, you may need to adjust the [AESlowCount] setting.

Settings Used: AESlowCount

Question: How do I use my Learner's Aura?

Answer: To use your Learner's Aura, set [DoLearners] to true in your PC's configuration. This will cause your PC to use the Learner's Aura instead of the Mana Regen Aura.

Settings Used: DoLearners

Question: Why am I not using the Aggro Rune?

Answer: The [DoAggroRune] setting determines whether or not your PC will cast the Tank Aggro Rune. If you are not using the Aggro Rune, you may need to Enable the [DoAggroRune] setting.

Settings Used: DoAggroRune

Question: Why am I not using NDT?

Answer: The [DoNDTBuff] setting determines whether or not your PC will cast the Night's Dark Terror Line. If you are not using NDT, you may need to Enable the [DoNDTBuff] setting.

Settings Used: DoNDTBuff

Question: Why am I not Slowing?

Answer: The [DoSlow] setting determines whether or not your PC will cast Slow spells. If you are not Slowing, you may need to Enable the [DoSlow] setting.

Settings Used: DoSlow

Question: Why am I not Tashing?

Answer: The [DoTash] setting determines whether or not your PC will cast Tash Spells. If you are not Tashing, you may need to Enable the [DoTash] setting.

Settings Used: DoTash

Question: I have Twincast AA why am I not using it on my DPS?

Answer: The [DoTwincastDPS] setting determines whether or not your PC will cast TwinCast DPS during the DPS rotation. If you have the Twincast AA and are not using it, you may need to Enable the [DoTwincastDPS] setting.

Settings Used: DoTwincastDPS

Question: Why am I not stripping buffs?

Answer: The [DoStripBuff] setting determines whether or not your PC will remove beneficial enemy effects. If you are not stripping buffs, you may need to Enable the [DoStripBuff] setting.

Settings Used: DoStripBuff

Question: Why am I not using Group Absorb?

Answer: The [DoGroupAbsorb] setting determines whether or not your PC will cast the Group Absorb line with -Hate Proc. If you are not using Group Absorb, you will revert to using single target rune spells instead.

Settings Used: DoGroupAbsorb

Question: Why am I not Crippling?

Answer: The [DoCripple] setting determines whether or not your PC will cast Cripple spells. If you are not Crippling, you may need to Enable the [DoCripple] setting.

Settings Used: DoCripple

Question: I have Twincast AA why am I not using it on my Mez?

Answer: The [DoTwincastMez] setting determines whether or not your PC will cast TwinCast Mez as your main ST Mez. If you have the Twincast AA and are not using it, you may need to Enable the [DoTwincastMez] setting.

Settings Used: DoTwincastMez

Question: What are the different Modes about?

Answer: The Default Mode is the original RGMercs configuration designed for levels 1 - 90. ModernEra Mode is a DPS rotation and spellset aimed at modern live play (~90+). The ModernEra Mode is designed to be used with the ModernEra DPS rotation and spellset. It is recommended to use the ModernEra Mode if you are level 90 or higher.

Settings Used: Mode

Question: Why am I not clicking my chest item?

Answer: The [DoChestClick] setting determines whether or not your PC will click your equipped chest item during burns. If you are not clicking your chest item, you may need to Enable the [DoChestClick] setting.

Settings Used: DoChestClick

Question: Why am I not using Group DoT Shield?

Answer: The [DoGroupDotShield] setting determines whether or not your PC will cast the Group DoT Shield Line. If you are not using Group DoT Shield, you may need to Enable the [DoGroupDotShield] setting.

Settings Used: DoGroupDotShield
