Answers

Question: How can I adjust when my characters all start following me?

Answer: Set [ChaseDistance] the distance you want them to start chasing, when you are outside of this range they will start to come your way.

Settings Used: ChaseDistance

Question: I have my Fellowship Insignia and want to use it, how do I do that?

Answer: Enable [DoFellow] and your characters will use the fellowship insignia automatically.

Settings Used: DoFellow

Question: I want to keep all of my characters within the same range for the camp, how do I do that?

Answer: Enabling [AutoCampRadius] will make all of your characters return to camp when they are this far away from their camp location.

Settings Used: AutoCampRadius

Question: I want to make sure my characters always have a campfire, how do I do that?

Answer: Enable [MaintainCampfire] and your characters will always have a campfire.You can select the type of campfire you want to use from the drop down.

Settings Used: MaintainCampfire

Question: How do I make my goobers not try and stand on top of me?

Answer: Set [ChaseStopDistance] to a higher number stop chasing you further away.

Settings Used: ChaseStopDistance

Question: How do I make my follow my driver?

Answer: Set the Driver to [ChaseTarget] and Turn on [ChaseOn] and you will follow your Chase Target.

Settings Used: ChaseOn

Question: I want to make sure my characters always follow me, even if I pause the main loop. How do I do that?

Answer: Enable [RunMovePaused] and your characters will follow you even if the main loop is paused.

Settings Used: RunMovePaused

Question: How do I tell my group who to follow?

Answer: Set the person to follow as the [ChaseTarget] and Turn on [ChaseOn] and you will follow your Chase Target.

Settings Used: ChaseTarget

Question: I want to make sure my characters always follow me, we can rebuff when we arrive. How do I do that?

Answer: Enable [PriorityFollow] and your characters will prioritize staying in range of the Chase Target over any other actions.

Settings Used: PriorityFollow

Question: I want to make sure my characters always have line of sight to their target, how do I do that?

Answer: Enable [RequireLoS] and your characters will try and maintain line of sight to their target when using NAV.

Settings Used: RequireLoS

Question: I died and my character started running back to my driver, how do I stop that?

Answer: Enable [BreakOnDeath] and your character will stop chasing when you die.

Settings Used: BreakOnDeath

Question: How do I make my characters return to camp after combat?

Answer: Enable [ReturnToCamp] and your characters will return to camp after combat.

Settings Used: ReturnToCamp

Question: I want to make sure my characters always return to camp, and always stay in the same positions within camp. How do I do that?

Answer: Enable [CampHard] and your characters will always return to camp after combat and in the same positions (loc) within camp.

Settings Used: CampHard
