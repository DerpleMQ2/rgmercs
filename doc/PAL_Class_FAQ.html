Answers

Question: Why am I not curing?

Answer: Make sure you have the [DoCures] setting enabled.

Settings Used: DoCures

Question: Why am I not clicking my chest item?

Answer: Make sure you have the [DoChestClick] setting enabled.

Settings Used: DoChestClick

Question: Why am I not casting Symbol?

Answer: Make sure you have the [DoDruid] setting enabled to use Symbol instead of Aego spells.

Settings Used: DoDruid

Question: Why am I not using Shield Flash?

Answer: You will want to adjust [FlashHP] to the % HP you have before using Shield Flash.

Settings Used: FlashHP

Question: Why am I not casting Reverse DS?

Answer: Make sure you have the [DoReverseDS] setting enabled.

Settings Used: DoReverseDS

Question: Why am I not swapping weapons?

Answer: Make sure you have the [DoBandolier] setting enabled. You will also need to have the bandolier setup in game.

Settings Used: DoBandolier

Question: Why am I not healing the target of my target?

Answer: You will want to adjust [TotHealPoint] to the % HP you have before using Target of Target heals.

Settings Used: TotHealPoint

Question: Why do I keep running out of arrows?

Answer: Make sure you have the [SummonArrows] setting enabled.

Settings Used: SummonArrows

Question: Why am I not using Lay on Hands?

Answer: You will want to adjust [LayHandsPct] to the % HP you have before using Lay on Hands.

Settings Used: LayHandsPct

Question: Why am I not casting my nukes?

Answer: Make sure you have the [DoNuke] setting enabled.

Settings Used: DoNuke

Question: Why am I not casting Brells?

Answer: Make sure you have the [DoBrells] setting enabled.

Settings Used: DoBrells

Question: What do the different Modes Do?

Answer: Tank Mode will focus on tanking and DPS Mode will focus on DPS.

Settings Used: Mode
