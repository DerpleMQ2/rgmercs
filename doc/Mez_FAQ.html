Answers

Question: I just learned my AA AOE Mez, Can I use that when its avail?

Answer: Certainly you can use your AA AOE Mez just Toggle [UseAEAAMez] on to use Area Effect AA Mez.

Settings Used: UseAEAAMez

Question: I keep trying to mez mobs that are too far away, how do I fix this?

Answer: Adnust your [MezRadius] to the distance you want to start mezzing mobs.

Settings Used: MezRadius

Question: Why do I keep mezzing the Grey con mobs?

Answer: You may have your [MezMinLevel] set too low, increase it to avoid mezzing grey con mobs.

Settings Used: MezMinLevel

Question: How do I control when to cast Mez?

Answer: You can adjust your [MezStartCount] to set how many mobs are on XTarget before casting Mez spells

Settings Used: MezStartCount

Question: My Character stops mezzing and there are still mobs in camp, why?

Answer: You may hae the [MaxMezCount] set too low, increase it to allow more mobs to be mezzed. (max value = 20)

Settings Used: MaxMezCount

Question: How do I set my AE mes limits?

Answer: Set your [MezAECount] Setting to the minimum number of Mobs on Xtarget before using AE Mez.

Settings Used: MezAECount

Question: I can't get my enchanter to mez mobs on hills, how do I fix this?

Answer: Adjust your [MezZRadius] to the height above/below you want to start mezzing mobs.

Settings Used: MezZRadius

Question: Why won't my enchanter mez this mob? His new spell should work on it.

Answer: You most likely have [AutoLevelRange] turned off and forgot to increase the [MezMaxLevel] to the max for this spell.

Settings Used: MezMaxLevel

Question: I keep trying to mez mobs that are about to die -- how do I fix this?

Answer: Adjust your [MezStopHPs] to the HP% you want to stop trying to mez mobs.

Settings Used: MezStopHPs

Question: I'm Lazy and hate updating my thresholds. How do I make my character do it for me?

Answer: Turning on [AutoLevelRange] will automatically adjust the level range for mezzing based on the spells you have.

Settings Used: AutoLevelRange

Question: How come my character is only using the AE Mez?

Answer: To use Single Target mez turn on [DoSTMez].

Settings Used: DoSTMez

Question: How do I turn on Mez?

Answer: Toggle [MezOn] to the on position.

Settings Used: MezOn
