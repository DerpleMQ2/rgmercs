Question: Why is my Shadow Knight Not using Corrupt Dot?

Answer: Make sure you have [DoCorruptionDot] enabled in your class settings.

Settings Used: DoCorruptionDot

Question: Why is my Shadow Knight Not using Dire Dot?

Answer: Make sure you have [DoDireDot] enabled in your class settings.

Settings Used: DoDireDot

Question: Why is my Shadow Knight Not using Torrents?

Settings Used: UseVoT

Question: Why is my Shadow Knight Not using Emergency Abilities?

Option: On critically low mana - will Only use when you are Crittically low on Mana, Option: As DD during burns - will use as a DD during burn phases, Option: For Mana or DD - will use for Mana or DD, depending on your current needs.
