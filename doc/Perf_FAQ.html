RGMercs Lua Edition: FAQ - Perf Module

Questions

  1. I want to see a longer span of time, can I adjust this?
  2. I want to see how long my modules are taking to run, how do I do that?
  3. Can I toggle between Lines and Bars?

Answers

Question: I want to see a longer span of time, can I adjust this?

Answer: Yes, you can adjust the number of [SecondsToStore] in the history with this setting.

Settings Used: SecondsToStore

Question: I want to see how long my modules are taking to run, how do I do that?

Answer: Enable [EnablePerfMonitoring] and you will see the performance of your modules in the Performance Monitor.

Settings Used: EnablePerfMonitoring

Question: Can I toggle between Lines and Bars?

Answer: Yes, Sort of. You can enable [PlotFillLines] and the graph will fill under the lines.

Settings Used: PlotFillLines
