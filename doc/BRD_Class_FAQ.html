Answers

Question: How do I use my Brass Instrument?

Answer: Set the [BrassInst] to use your Brass Instrument when you are in combat. And make sure you have [SwapInstruments] enabled.

Settings Used: BrassInst

Question: How do I use my Symphony of Battle items?

Answer: You can enable [UseSoBItems] to use your Symphony of Battle items when you are in combat.

Settings Used: UseSoBItems

Question: How do I use my Wind Instrument?

Answer: Set the [WindInst] to use your Wind Instrument when you are in combat. And make sure you have [SwapInstruments] enabled.

Settings Used: WindInst

Question: How do I use my Fierce Eye AA?

Answer: You can enable [UseFierceEye] to use your Fierce Eye AA when you are in combat. Options are to use it only during burns, always, or never.

Settings Used: UseFierceEye

Question: How do I use my Dreadstone?

Answer: You can enable [UseDreadstone] to use your Dreadstone when you are able.

Settings Used: UseDreadstone

Question: How do I use my single target Slow song?

Answer: You can enable [DoSTSlow] to use your single target Slow song when the target is not already slowed.

Settings Used: DoSTSlow

Question: How do I use my AE Slow song?

Answer: You can enable [DoAESlow] to use your AE Slow song when the target is not already slowed.

Settings Used: DoAESlow

Question: How do I use my Runspeed Buff?

Answer: You can enable [UseRunBuff] to use your Runspeed Buff. Options are (AA, Song (Long Duration Only), Song (Fastest Available), Off).

Settings Used: UseRunBuff

Question: How do I use my Dispel song?

Answer: You can enable [DoDispel] to use your Dispel song when the target has beneficial effects.

Settings Used: DoDispel

Question: How do I make sure we put back the correct item after using an instrument?

Answer: Set the [Offhand] to use your Offhand item when you are not in combat or don't need an instrument. And make sure you have [SwapInstruments] enabled.

Settings Used: Offhand

Question: How do I use my Suffering song?

Answer: You can enable [UseProcSong] to use your Suffering song.Options are (Never, In-Combat Only, Always, Out-of-Combat Only).

Settings Used: UseProcSong

Question: How do I use my Fire Spell Buff song?

Settings Used: UseFireBuff

Question: How do I use my Crescendo song?

Settings Used: UseCrescendo

Question: Why am I not using my Crescendo or Reflexive Strike songs?

Answer: You group may be above your [GroupManaPct] setting in group mana. Try adjusting this to the maximum amount of mana you want your group to have before using these abilities.

Settings Used: GroupManaPct

Answer: You may not have enough party members under the [GroupManaPct] setting in group mana. Try adjusting this to the number of party members you want to have below [GroupManaPct] before using these abilities.

Question: What do the different Modes do?

Answer: There are four modes: General, Tank Caster and Healer. General is the default mode and will prioritize your gems for general use. Tank will prioritize your gems for tanking abilities. Caster will prioritize your gems for casting abilities. Healer will prioritize your gems for healing abilities.

Settings Used: Mode
