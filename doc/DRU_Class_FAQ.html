Answers

Question: Why am I not DOTing?

Answer: Make sure [DoDot] is enabled. If you are in Heal Mode, you may not be DOTing.

Settings Used: DoDot

Question: Can I choose between Fire or Ice based Nukes?

Answer: Yes, you can choose between Fire and Ice based Nukes by toggling [DoFire]. When [DoFire] is enabled, we will use Fire based Nukes. When [DoFire] is disabled, we will use Ice based Nukes.

Settings Used: DoFire

Question: Why am I nuking at 10% health?

Answer: Make sure [NukePct] is set to the correct health percentage you want to start nuking at.

Settings Used: NukePct

Question: I have Twincastig AA, can I use it?

Answer: Yes, you can enable [DoTwinHeal] to use Twin Heal Nuke spells.

Settings Used: DoTwinHeal

Question: Why am I not Snaring?

Answer: Make sure [DoSnare] is enabled. If you are in Heal Mode, you may not be snaring.

Settings Used: DoSnare

Question: I like Rain spells, can I use them?

Answer: Yes, you can enable [DoRain] to use Rain spells.

Settings Used: DoRain

Question: Sometimes I group with a bard and don't need to worry about Run Speed, can I disable it?

Answer: Yes, you can disable [DoRunSpeed] to prevent casting Run Speed spells.

Settings Used: DoRunSpeed

Question: Why am I not clicking my chest item?

Answer: Make sure [DoChestClick] is enabled. If you are in Heal Mode, you may not be clicking your chest item.

Settings Used: DoChestClick

Question: What do the different Modes Do?

Answer: Heal Mode will focus on healing and buffing. Mana Mode will focus on DPS and Mana Management.

Settings Used: Mode

Question: Why am I not Nuking?

Answer: Make sure [DoNuke] is enabled. If you are in Heal Mode, you may not be nuking. Also double check [NukePct] to ensure you are nuking at the correct health percentage.

Settings Used: DoNuke
