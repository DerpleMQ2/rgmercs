Answers

Question: Why am I not using Battle Leap?

Answer: Make sure you have [DoBattleLeap] enabled.

Settings Used: DoBattleLeap

Question: Why am I not using AoE?

Answer: Make sure you have [DoAoe] enabled.

Settings Used: DoAoe

Question: What do the different modes do?

Answer: Currently Berserkers Only have DPS mode. More modes will be added in the future.

Settings Used: Mode

Question: Why am I not using Intimidate?

Answer: Make sure you have [DoIntimidate] enabled. Early levels of Intimidate can fear the target. USE WITH CAUTION!

Settings Used: DoIntimidate

Question: Why am I not summoning Axes?

Answer: Make sure you have [SummonAxes] enabled.

Settings Used: SummonAxes

Question: Why am I not summoning Dicho Axes?

Answer: Make sure you have [SummonDichoAxes] enabled.

Settings Used: SummonDichoAxes

Question: Why am I not using my Epic clicky?

Answer: Make sure you have [DoEpic] enabled.

Settings Used: DoEpic

Question: I keep running out of Axes, what do I do?

Answer: Increase the [AutoAxeCount] to summon more Axes when you hit this threshold remaining.

Settings Used: AutoAxeCount

Question: Why am I not using Disconcerting Discipline?

Answer: Make sure you have [DoDisconDisc] enabled.

Settings Used: DoDisconDisc

Question: Why am I not using my opener?

Answer: Make sure you have [DoOpener] enabled.

Settings Used: DoOpener

Question: I keep running out of Dicho Axes, what do I do?

Answer: Increase the [DichoAxeCount] to summon more Dicho Axes when you hit this threshold remaining.

Settings Used: DichoAxeCount
