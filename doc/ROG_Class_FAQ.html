Answers

Question: How can I make sure to always be sneaking / hiding for maximum DPS?

Answer: Enable [DoHideSneak] if you want to use Hide/Sneak during Downtime. This will keep you ready to ambush or backstab at a moments notice.

Settings Used: DoHideSneak

Question: I am always summoning more poison, how can I make sure to summon enough the first time?

Answer: You can set the minimum number of poisons you want to have before summoning more by setting the [PoisonItemCount] field.

Settings Used: PoisonItemCount

Question: Why would I not want to click my epic item?

Answer: Enable [DoEpic] if you want to use the epic item.

Settings Used: DoEpic

Question: Why would I not want to use Openers?

Answer: Enable [DoOpener] if you want to use the opener ability.

Settings Used: DoOpener

Question: I want to use a clicky item to summon my poisons, how do I do that?

Answer: You can set your poison summoner by placing its name into the [PoisonClicky] field. You can drag and drop the poison summoner item from your inventory into the field.

Settings Used: PoisonClicky

Question: Why would I not want to click my chest item?

Answer: Enable [DoChestClick] if you want to use the chest item.

Settings Used: DoChestClick

Question: What do the different Modes do?

Answer: Currently Rogues only have DPS mode, this may change in the future

Settings Used: Mode

Question: Can I specify the poison I want to use?

Answer: Yes you set your poison item by placing its name into the [PoisonName] field. You can drag and drop the poison item from your inventory into the field.

Settings Used: PoisonName
